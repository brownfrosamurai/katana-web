# Overview

Spend Lens is a bank statement analyzer that helps users make sense of their financial data. Upload statements, and the app parses and visualizes spending patterns to support better money decisions.

![Spend Lens](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1554224155-6726b3ff858f?w=800&q=80)

## Approach

Built to simplify personal finance tracking. Parse bank statements, group transactions, and surface insights so users can see where their money goes.

## Features

- Bank statement parsing
- Spending categorization
- Transaction analytics
